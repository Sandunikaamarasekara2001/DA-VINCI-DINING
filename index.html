Aout Us

Our History

Welcome to Da Vinci Dining, where culinary artistry meets a rich legacy of tradition and innovation. Since our inception in 2010, Da Vinci Dining has been dedicated to offering an unparalleled dining experience inspired by the timeless genius of Leonardo da Vinci.

Our Beginnings

Founded by Mrs.Amarasekara, a passionate chef with a vision, Da Vinci Dining was born from the desire to blend the art of fine dining with the spirit of creativity and exploration. What started as a small bistro in the heart of Kandy has grown into a beloved establishment known for its exquisite cuisine and warm, inviting atmosphere.

A Culinary Journey

Drawing inspiration from the Renaissance era, our menu is a masterpiece that celebrates both classic and contemporary flavors. Our chefs are committed to sourcing the finest ingredients, ensuring every dish reflects the artistry and attention to detail that define Da Vinci Dining. From hand-crafted pastas to innovative seafood creations, our culinary offerings are a tribute to the enduring legacy of Leonardo da Vinci.

Commitment to Excellence

Throughout the years, our dedication to excellence has remained unwavering. We continually strive to push the boundaries of culinary creativity, offering our guests an experience that is as delightful to the palate as it is to the senses. Our team, from the kitchen to the front of house, shares a passion for providing exceptional service and creating memorable dining experiences.

Looking to the Future

As we look to the future, Da Vinci Dining remains committed to evolving and enhancing our offerings while staying true to our roots. We invite you to join us on this journey, where each visit promises a new discovery and every meal is a celebration of the art of dining.
